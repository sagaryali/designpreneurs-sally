Our
People

Behind every great hackathon is a community of people who believe design can change how the world builds. Our mentors, judges, and speakers bring that belief — and years of hard-won experience — into the room.

They come from the companies shaping AI, the studios defining interaction design, and the startups proving that taste is a competitive advantage. They mentor because they remember when someone did it for them.

This is the network that makes Designpreneurs more than a weekend — it's the reason participants leave with new ways of thinking, and sometimes, new careers.
