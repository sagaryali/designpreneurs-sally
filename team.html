People

We're a community of design-centered builders and entrepreneurs who believe the best products start with people who care how things feel — not just how they function.

Most hackathons reward the fastest demo. We reward the sharpest idea, well-made. In our hackathon, you'll go from a blank canvas to a working product, with mentorship, design critique, and the kind of room where good taste compounds.

Designpreneurs is where design becomes a business. Where the person who sweats the details also pitches the vision.

Designpreneurs was born because the gap between "designer" and "founder" should be a weekend, not a career. And we're just getting started.

Founder's Letter

I didn't set out to build a hackathon. I set out to solve one problem for my design students.

I teach interaction and startup design at Parsons and SVA, and I kept seeing the same gap. My students were brilliant on the screen — beautiful interfaces, sharp instincts — but they'd never shipped anything into the real world. No exposure to real users, real constraints, real stakes. So I gave them a challenge: one weekend, take an idea from blank canvas to a working product. I just wanted them to walk out with a portfolio piece.

Then I saw what was happening on the other side. The products were finding real product-market fit. Founders in the room were learning to think design-first instead of leading with a business model — and their ideas got sharper for it. The weekend I built to teach a portfolio turned into something that drives real business impact.

Since then, one startup from this community raised $4M. Another was acquired. More than 70 designers landed jobs through the platform. We've stayed small and selective on purpose — every sponsor, judge, mentor, and hacker is hand-picked — because quality compounds and the room is everything.

I believe there should be far more designer-founders in the world. The gap between "designer" and "founder" should be a weekend, not a career. That's what we're here to close.

— Sally Chung, Founder, Designpreneurs
